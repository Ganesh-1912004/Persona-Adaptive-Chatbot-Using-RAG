The Pimpri Chinchwad College of Engineering (PCCOE) was established in 1999.
It is located in Nigdi, Pune, India. The college is part of the Pimpri Chinchwad Education Trust (PCET).
The motto of the college is "Knowledge Brings Freedom".

PCCOE offers undergraduate programs in various engineering disciplines, including Computer Engineering,
Mechanical Engineering, Electronics & Telecommunication, and Information Technology.
It also offers postgraduate programs.

The college is known for its strong focus on industry-academia interaction, research, and project-based learning.
PCCOE has received numerous awards and rankings for its academic excellence and infrastructure.
The campus is equipped with modern laboratories, a central library, and sports facilities.

Admissions to undergraduate courses are primarily based on scores from the Maharashtra Health and Technical Common Entrance Test (MHT-CET)
and the Joint Entrance Examination (JEE) Main.

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering (AI & ML) is a new and advanced department focusing on cutting-edge technologies.
The curriculum is designed to provide students with a strong foundation in artificial intelligence, machine learning,
data science, and deep learning. Students in this department work on innovative projects, such as the
"Persona-Adaptive Conversational AI", which aims to create chatbots that can understand and adapt to user behavior.
This project utilizes technologies like LangChain for orchestrating AI workflows and Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)
to provide context-aware and factually accurate responses.
